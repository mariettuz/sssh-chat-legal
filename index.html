Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

sssh-chat is a privacy-focused end-to-end encrypted messaging application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: username and hashed password (we never store your password in plain text).
  • Cryptographic keys: public keys used for the Signal Protocol end-to-end encryption. Your private keys never leave your device.
  • Messages: messages are stored on our servers only to enable delivery. They are end-to-end encrypted and unreadable to us. Ephemeral messages are automatically deleted from our servers within approximately 30 days.
  • Media files: images and files you send are stored encrypted on Cloudflare R2 object storage.
  • Device token: a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver silent push notifications. No message content is ever sent through FCM.

2. How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used exclusively to provide the messaging service. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the application.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare R2: used for encrypted media storage.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): used to deliver push notification signals. No message content is transmitted through this service.

4. Data Retention

  • Ephemeral messages are automatically deleted from our servers within approximately 30 days.
  • Account data is retained until you request deletion.
  • Media files are retained until the associated message is deleted or you request account deletion.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To do so, contact us at the email address below. We will process your request within 30 days.

6. Security

All messages are end-to-end encrypted using the Signal Protocol. This means only you and your intended recipients can read your messages. We have no technical ability to access message content.

7. Children's Privacy

sssh-chat is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the application. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
